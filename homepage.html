3-gatsu no lion

Rei Kiriyama Shimada kai Akira Hinata Momo

Rei kiriyama

Rei Kiriyama (桐山 零, Kiriyama Rei) is the main protagonist whose progression is explored through the series. He is a teenage professional shogi player, currently ranked at 5th-dan (6th dan in the later part of the manga). He is widely lauded in the shogi community as the fifth shogi player to become a professional in middle school. Using his salary combined with his life savings, he lives in own apartment while juggling between his official matches and high school. By the time the story begins, Rei is also a frequent visitor of the Kawamoto residence where the family treats him as one of their own despite his initial reluctance to become close with them.
